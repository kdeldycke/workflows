### Description

Losslessly optimizes PNG and JPEG images using [oxipng](https://github.com/shssoichiro/oxipng) and [jpegoptim](https://github.com/tjko/jpegoptim). See the [`format-images` job documentation](https://kdeldycke.github.io/repomatic/workflows.html#github-workflows-autofix-yaml-jobs) for details.
