### Description

Losslessly optimizes PNG and JPEG images using [oxipng](https://github.com/shssoichiro/oxipng) and [jpegoptim](https://github.com/tjko/jpegoptim). See the [`format-images` job documentation](https://github.com/kdeldycke/repomatic?tab=readme-ov-file#githubworkflowsautofixyaml-jobs) for details.
