### Description

Ready to be merged into `main` branch, at the discretion of the maintainers, to bump the \$part part of the version number. Version bumps are scheduled daily and also triggered after releases. See the [`bump-versions` job documentation](https://github.com/kdeldycke/repokit?tab=readme-ov-file#githubworkflowschangelogyaml-jobs) for details.
