### Description

Syncs the local `renovate.json5` with the canonical reference from [`kdeldycke/workflows`](https://github.com/kdeldycke/workflows/blob/main/gha_utils/data/renovate.json5). Repo-specific settings (`customManagers`, `assignees`) are stripped. See the [`sync-renovate` job documentation](https://github.com/kdeldycke/workflows?tab=readme-ov-file#githubworkflowsautofixyaml-jobs) for details.
